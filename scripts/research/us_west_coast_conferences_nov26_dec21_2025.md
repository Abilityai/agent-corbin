## Executive Summary

This comprehensive research report identifies 20+ conferences, networking events, and business opportunities across the US West Coast from November 26 - December 21, 2025. The landscape is dominated by major technology events in Las Vegas and San Francisco during the first two weeks of December, with strong opportunities for AI/ML, cloud computing, and investor networking. Key findings include:

- **AWS re:Invent** (Dec 1-5, Las Vegas) is the anchor event with 1,000+ sessions and extensive AI/cloud focus [1]
- **Fortune Brainstorm AI** (Dec 8-9, SF) offers exclusive access to 300 senior AI executives and decision-makers [2]
- **NeurIPS 2025** (Dec 2-7, San Diego) is the premier academic AI/ML conference with cutting-edge research [3]
- Strong investor/startup networking scene throughout December in SF and LA [4, 5]
- Thanksgiving week (Nov 27-29) has minimal activity; active period begins Dec 1 [6]

The trip timing aligns well with major decision-making periods (Q4 budgets) and offers multiple high-value touchpoints with enterprise buyers, technical leaders, and investors across AI automation and agentic AI solutions.
