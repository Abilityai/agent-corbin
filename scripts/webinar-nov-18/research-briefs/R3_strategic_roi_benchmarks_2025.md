# Research Brief R3: Strategic ROI Benchmarks for AI Implementations (2025)

## Research Objective
Find ACTUAL ROI data from AI implementations in 2025 - not projections or promises, but measured results. Position AbilityAI case studies (271% ROI, $105K savings, etc.) in context of industry benchmarks.

## Target Timeframe
**2025 data only** - ROI results published/reported in 2025 (may be from 2024 implementations)

## Key Research Questions

1. **What ROI ranges are companies actually achieving with AI in 2025?**
   - By use case type (sales automation, knowledge systems, content generation, process automation)
   - By company size (SMB, mid-market, enterprise)
   - By industry (where data exists)

2. **What defines "world-class" vs. "typical" vs. "underperforming" AI ROI in 2025?**
   - Top quartile ROI: what range?
   - Median ROI: what range?
   - Bottom quartile ROI: what range?
   - What separates top performers from the rest?

3. **How do AbilityAI case study numbers compare to industry benchmarks?**
   - **271% ROI (BeaverCraft Sales)** - exceptional or typical for sales automation?
   - **$105K savings, 3x capacity (Lead-to-Proposal)** - exceptional or typical for process automation?
   - **$100-150K savings, 84% quality, 95% faster (EV Energy Knowledge)** - exceptional or typical for knowledge systems?
   - **70-80% time reduction (Holywater Documentation)** - exceptional or typical for content generation?

4. **What payback periods are companies actually experiencing in 2025?**
   - Quick wins (< 6 months payback)
   - Medium term (6-12 months payback)
   - Long term (12+ months payback)
   - Which use cases deliver fastest ROI?

5. **What investment levels produce what ROI outcomes?**
   - ROI by investment tier (< $50K, $50K-$250K, $250K+)
   - Cost-to-value ratios
   - What does "good ROI" look like at different budget levels?

6. **What separates high-ROI implementations from low-ROI implementations?**
   - Company characteristics
   - Implementation approaches
   - Use case selection criteria
   - Organizational readiness factors

7. **How are companies measuring AI ROI in 2025?**
   - What metrics are standard?
   - Financial metrics (cost savings, revenue gains, ROI %)
   - Operational metrics (time savings, capacity gains, quality improvements)
   - How do leading companies attribute value to AI?

8. **What ROI trends are emerging from 2024 to 2025?**
   - Are ROI expectations changing?
   - Are actual results improving or declining?
   - GenAI ROI vs. traditional AI ROI

## Priority Data Sources

**High Priority:**
- Gartner AI ROI benchmarks 2025
- McKinsey AI economic impact studies 2025
- Forrester AI ROI research 2025
- IDC AI business value studies
- MIT Sloan AI ROI case studies
- Deloitte AI value realization research

**Medium Priority:**
- Industry analyst firm reports (specific to use cases)
- CFO Magazine AI investment returns
- CIO Magazine ROI case studies
- Earnings call mentions of AI ROI (public companies)
- Consulting firm benchmarking data

**Use Case-Specific:**
- Sales automation ROI benchmarks
- Knowledge management/RAG system ROI
- Content generation ROI
- Process automation ROI

**Avoid:**
- Vendor-published ROI case studies (biased, cherry-picked)
- Projected/estimated ROI (want actual measured results)
- Pre-2024 data (landscape has changed with GenAI)

Produce a markdown document with:

1. **Executive Summary** (key findings)
   - What "good ROI" looks like in 2025
   - How AbilityAI numbers compare
   - Major ROI trends

- **VCs** need to evaluate portfolio company AI ROI claims
- **Enterprise Leaders** need to benchmark their results against peers
