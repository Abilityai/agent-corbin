# Research Brief R1: CEO and Board AI Priorities in 2025

## Research Objective
Identify what CEOs and boards are ACTUALLY prioritizing with AI in 2025 - not what vendors are selling, but what executive leadership is asking about, approving, and focusing on.

## Target Timeframe
**2025 data only** - Q4 2024 through present (November 2025)

## Key Research Questions

1. **What are the top 5-7 AI priorities for CEOs in 2025?**
   - Based on CEO surveys from Gartner, McKinsey, Deloitte, PwC
   - Board meeting agenda items related to AI
   - CEO earnings call mentions and themes

2. **How have CEO AI priorities shifted from 2024 to 2025?**
   - What dropped off the priority list?
   - What emerged as new priorities?
   - Why the shift?

3. **What strategic questions are boards asking management about AI?**
   - Governance and oversight questions
   - Risk and compliance questions
   - ROI and investment questions
   - Competitive positioning questions

4. **What separates companies with AI as a top-3 priority vs. lower priority?**
   - Industry patterns
   - Company size patterns
   - What triggers AI to become urgent?

5. **What decision criteria are CEOs using to approve/reject AI investments?**
   - ROI thresholds
   - Risk tolerance levels
   - Strategic alignment requirements
   - Competitive necessity factors

6. **What are CEOs saying "no" to in 2025 regarding AI?**
   - Which AI initiatives are being rejected?
   - Why are they being rejected?
   - What objections are most common?

7. **What level of CEO involvement in AI strategy is typical in 2025?**
   - Hands-on vs. delegated
   - CEO as champion vs. skeptic
   - Board oversight models

## Priority Data Sources

**High Priority:**
- Gartner CEO Survey 2025
- McKinsey C-Suite AI Survey 2025
- Deloitte CEO Perspectives 2025
- PwC CEO Survey 2025
- Harvard Business Review CEO interviews (2025)
- Fortune 500 earnings call transcripts mentioning AI (Q4 2024 - Q3 2025)
- WSJ, FT CEO interviews about AI strategy

**Medium Priority:**
- CFO Research AI investment surveys
- Board member perspectives on AI governance
- CEO podcasts and long-form interviews
- Industry-specific CEO surveys

**Avoid:**
- Vendor white papers and case studies (biased)
- AI researcher opinions (too technical, not executive focus)
- Generalist AI trend pieces (not executive-specific)

## Deliverable Format

Produce a markdown document with:

1. **Executive Summary** (3-5 key findings)
   - What CEOs are prioritizing in 2025
   - Major shifts from 2024
   - Strategic implications

2. **Top 5-7 CEO AI Priorities for 2025** (ranked by frequency/importance)
   - Each priority with: description, why it matters, supporting data

3. **Board-Level AI Questions** (categorized)
   - Governance questions
   - Risk questions
   - ROI questions
   - Strategic questions

4. **Decision Criteria Analysis**
   - What makes CEOs say "yes" vs. "no"
   - ROI thresholds and expectations
   - Risk tolerance patterns

5. **Strategic Implications for Webinar**
   - How to position the "3 gaps" framework against these priorities
   - Which CEO concerns your framework addresses
   - Language that resonates with 2025 CEO mindset

6. **Quotable Insights** (5-10 CEO quotes about AI from 2025)

7. **Source List** (all sources with dates and links)

## Why This Research Matters for the Webinar

**For High-Value Participants:**
- **CEO Coaches** need to know what their CEO clients are being asked by boards
- **VCs** need to understand what portfolio company boards should be asking
- **Enterprise Leaders** need to benchmark their AI priorities against peers

**For the Presentation:**
- Opens with "Here's what CEOs are ACTUALLY asking about AI in 2025"
- Positions the "3 gaps" as answering the real CEO questions
- Builds credibility by showing you understand the executive mindset
- Helps tailor messaging to resonate with strategic decision-makers

## Success Criteria

Research is successful if it provides:
- ✅ Clear picture of 2025 CEO AI priorities (data-driven, not speculation)
- ✅ Evidence of priority shifts from 2024 to 2025
- ✅ Actionable insights on CEO decision criteria
- ✅ 5-10 quotable CEO insights from 2025
- ✅ Strategic framing for the webinar positioning
